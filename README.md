# 📊 Superstore Sales Dashboard

## 🚀 Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing sales performance using the Superstore dataset.  
An interactive dashboard was created to understand key business metrics such as revenue, profit, and sales trends.

The goal is to convert raw data into meaningful insights for better decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze overall sales performance
- Identify top-performing categories and regions
- Track monthly sales trends
- Highlight key business insights

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features
- ✅ Total Sales
- ✅ Total Profit
- ✅ Total Quantity
- ✅ Profit Margin
- ✅ Sales by Category
- ✅ Sales by Region
- ✅ Top 10 Products by Sales
- ✅ Monthly Sales Trend

---

## 🛠 Tools Used
- Microsoft Excel / Power BI
- Pivot Tables
- Pivot Charts / Visualizations
- Data Cleaning & Transformation

---

## 🔍 Key Insights
- 📌 Technology category generates the highest sales
- 📌 West region contributes the highest revenue
- 📌 Certain products drive major sales contribution
- 📌 Sales show a consistent growth trend over time
